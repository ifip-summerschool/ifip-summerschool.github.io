Please note that for each accepted abstract, at least one (co-)author has to attend the summer school and register by the early-bird registration deadline.

| Registration type | Early bird<sup>1</sup> | Late registration<sup>1</sup> |
| -- | -- | -- |
| Regular registration  | 500€ | 550€ |
| IFIP Member registration<sup>2</sup> | 450€ | 500€ |

<sup>1</sup> Early bird registration fees apply until June 19.<br />
<sup>2</sup> All individual members of IFIP member societies as well as all members of IFIP Technical Committees (TCs) and Working Groups (WGs) are entitled to a discount of 10% on registration fees. To claim the discount, you will need to state which IFIP member society/TC/WG you belong to during the registration process.

Please register using the following link: [https://www.law.kuleuven.be/apps/activiteiten/users/subscriptions/create?activityId=6687](https://www.law.kuleuven.be/apps/activiteiten/users/subscriptions/create?activityId=6687)

#### Important information
1. If it's your first time that you attend an event with us, the tool will require you to create an account in order to register. We cannot register you manually.
2. The registration tool can be switched to Dutch or English in the upper right corner.
3. After registration, we will see an overview page of your registration  option(s), make sure to confirm (or edit) at the bottom of this overview page. Only then your registration is confirmed by email.
4. Payment (with credit card or via bank tranfer) can only be done after receiving the invoice.
5. The invoice will be processed by the Finance Department and will probably take some time before you receive it. The email confirmation is the proof of registration.

In case of any questions, please contact us via e-mail [law.citip.admin@kuleuven.be](mailto:law.citip.admin@kuleuven.be).
